Privacy Policy for QuickAI - Developer Toolkit

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. Introduction

QuickAI - Developer Toolkit ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

QuickAI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Health information
  • Personal communications
  • Website content you view or interact with

3. How the Extension Works

QuickAI operates entirely locally on your device. All functionality is contained within the extension package and does not communicate with external servers or third-party services.

4. Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure within the extension are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

QuickAI does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties because we do not collect any user data.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickAI does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the extension at any time through your Chrome browser settings.

10. Compliance

QuickAI complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Google's Privacy Policy requirements
  • Applicable data protection regulations
